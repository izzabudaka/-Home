Intel IoT Roadshow Judging Criteria:
1. How effectively does the Project Submission maximises and innovate upon the Dev Kit? (up to 50 points)
2. How effectivelly does the Project Submission make use of the following hardware and software components of the Dev Kit?
(total of up to 40 points):
- Cloud connectivity (up to 10 points)
- Sensor utilization (up to 10 points)
- Overall Dev Kit utilization (up to 10 points)
- Other technical aspects (up to 10 points), and
3. How effectively does the Project Submission present a compelling solution and show potential for market viability once 
fully developed? (up to 30 points).
